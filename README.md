# Groove Pal

**A pal to whom you send your groove.**

---

## What Is This?

Groove Pal is a vinyl sharing network built on trust, patience, and presence. It's what happens when you combine the intimacy of a penpal with the soul of a record collection — and let it travel.

You receive a record. You listen — really listen. You attach your sticker. You send it to someone new. The record travels. The connection grows.

No algorithms. No feeds. Just music, mail, and meaning.

---

## How It Works

### The Journey of a Record

1. **You join** — Choose your entry: Standard ($45, 2 vinyls) or Solo ($35, 1 vinyl)
2. **You receive** — Your welcome vinyl arrives with QR stickers and a branded sleeve
3. **You check in** — Scan the QR code on your record to activate your Pal status
4. **You listen** — Sit with someone, put on the record, be present
5. **You attach your sticker** — Your QR code joins the record's story
6. **You send forward** — Mail it to a friend, family member, or stranger
7. **They scan and register** — When they check in, you become an Active Pal
8. **The cycle continues** — Active Pals are eligible to receive records from the network

### The Active Pal System

**To receive records from the network, you must be an Active Pal.**

You become Active when:
- You ship a record forward
- The recipient scans the QR and checks in

This ensures records actually travel — they don't just accumulate on shelves. The network rewards participation, not just payment.

### The Sticker System

Each Groove Pal receives a sheet of personal QR code stickers when they join. The sticker represents:

- Your identity in the network
- Proof you touched this record
- An invitation for the next person to join

On the record sleeve, stickers accumulate like stamps in a passport. Each scan reveals who sent it, when, and their note about the music. The record tells its own story through the people it's visited.

## Shipping Made Easy

We know shipping a record is a hassle. So we made it simple:

1. **Click "Send Forward"** on the platform
2. **Pay for shipping** — Integrated checkout (Apple Pay, Stripe)
3. **Generate QR shipping label** — Pre-paid USPS Media Mail label
4. **Print and attach** — Standard label + Groove Pal branding
5. **Drop off or schedule pickup** — USPS handles the rest

Cheap, easy, and trackable. The recipient scans the QR on arrival, and you're confirmed as an Active Pal.

## The Philosophy

In a world of infinite scroll and instant everything, Groove Pal is intentionally slow. It takes days for a record to arrive. It takes time to sit down and listen. It takes effort to write a note and mail it forward.

That's the point.

Every friction is a feature. Every delay is a gift. The wait makes the arrival meaningful. The physicality makes the connection real.

**We believe:**
- Music is meant to be shared, not hoarded
- Patience is a form of presence
- Trust can be built between strangers
- Stories travel better on vinyl

## The Records

The records in circulation span decades, genres, and levels of notoriety. You might discover an unknown artist who becomes your new favorite. Or dive deeper into an artist you "know" — but really only "know of."

## Who This Is For

Groove Pal is for people who:
- Remember when mixtapes meant something
- Miss the ritual of music
- Want connection that isn't mediated by algorithms
- Believe in trust between strangers
- Have records they love — and want to share

It's not for collectors who want to preserve value. It's not for completists who need every pressing. It's for people who understand that music gets better when it travels, and records gain meaning from the people who've loved them.

## The Platform

**groovepal.com** is where the digital meets the physical:

- **Check in** — Scan your record's QR to activate
- **Track your records** — See where the vinyl you've sent has traveled
- **Discover stories** — Read notes from the people who held it before you
- **Say thanks** — Send gratitude to the pal who sent you a record
- **Send forward** — Generate shipping labels and log your shipments
- **Active Pal status** — Track your eligibility to receive network records

The platform is minimal by design. It exists to support the physical experience, not replace it.
