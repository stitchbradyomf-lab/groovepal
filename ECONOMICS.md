# Groove Pal — Economics (Revised April 2026)

**The math behind the meaning.**

---

## The Core Model

Groove Pal operates on a simple economic premise: charge enough to cover costs, reward sharing, and sustain the network — without extracting more than necessary.

This isn't venture-scale economics. It's sustainable economics. The goal is a healthy, growing network that pays for itself and rewards the people who make it work.

---

## Two Entry Tiers

### Standard Entry — $45

**What You Get:**

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 2 Vinyl Records | One for you, one for a Pal you identify |
| QR Sticker Sheet | Your personal stickers (mailed to you) |
| Branded Outer Sleeve | Simple branded protector for your records |
| Platform Access | Track records, check in, earn Active Pal status |
| Shipping | Included to you (you handle shipping to your Pal) |

**The Philosophy:** The 2-vinyl model grows the network immediately. You're not just joining — you're bringing someone with you. This promotes generosity and accelerates network effects.

### Solo Entry — $35

**What You Get:**

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 1 Vinyl Record | Curated selection for you |
| QR Sticker Sheet | Your personal stickers (mailed to you) |
| Branded Outer Sleeve | Simple branded protector |
| Platform Access | Full access, but you're not yet on Active Pal list |

**The Path to Active:** Once you send your record forward and the recipient scans in, you join the Active Pal list and become eligible to receive records from the network.

**The Philosophy:** Lower barrier to entry, higher margin per user. We encourage the $45 tier because it grows the network, but the $35 tier captures those who need a lower entry point.

## The Active Pal System

### How It Works

**To join the Active Pal list:**
1. Receive a record (via $45 Standard, $35 Solo, or gift)
2. Ship it forward to someone new
3. That person scans the QR code and checks in
4. You're now an Active Pal — eligible to receive records from the network

**Why This Matters:**
- Ensures records actually travel (not just accumulate)
- Rewards participation, not just payment
- Creates natural scarcity (only Active Pals receive)
- Builds trust through demonstrated action

### Active Pal Economics

**Cost to acquire an Active Pal:**
- Standard tier: $30-38 (immediate, if they ship)
- Solo tier: $22-31 (delayed, only after they ship)

**Value of an Active Pal:**
- Brings in new members (via shipping)
- Maintains record circulation
- Creates network density
- Generates stories/content

## Shipping Integration

### The Flow

1. **Pal decides to ship** → Clicks "Send Forward" on platform
2. **Pays for shipping** → Integrated checkout (Apple Pay, Stripe)
3. **Generates QR shipping label** → Pre-paid Media Mail label with tracking
4. **Prints and attaches** → Standard USPS label + Groove Pal branding
5. **Drops off or schedules pickup** → USPS handles delivery
6. **Recipient scans QR** → Checks in, sender becomes/confirmed Active Pal

### Shipping Economics

**USPS Media Mail Rates (2026 estimates):**
- 1-2 lbs (single record): $4.50-5.50
- 2-3 lbs (double record): $5.50-6.50
- Insurance: +$2-3

**Platform markup:** 10-15% to cover integration costs
- User pays: $5-7 per shipment
- Our cost: $4.50-6.50
- Margin: $0.50-1.50 per shipment

**Volume discounts:** At 100+ shipments/month, negotiate commercial rates

## Cash Flow Considerations

### Upfront Nature

Groove Pal has favorable cash flow dynamics:
- **Cash in:** Payment received immediately at signup
- **Cash out:** Fulfillment costs within 1-2 weeks
- **No ongoing subscription:** No monthly churn to manage
- **Active Pal liability:** Builds slowly, predictably

### Working Capital Needs

| Phase | Inventory Needed | Cash Required |
|-------|------------------|---------------|
| Founding (10 pals) | ~$150 vinyl | ~$400 |
| Early (100 pals) | ~$1,000 vinyl | ~$2,500 |
| Growth (500 pals) | ~$4,000 vinyl | ~$10,000 |
| Scale (1,000+ pals) | ~$8,000+ vinyl | ~$20,000+ |

### Inventory Strategy

- **Start small:** Source 25-50 records via deals/curated selection
- **Curate carefully:** Each record should be worth sharing
- **Diversify:** Mix of genres, decades, accessibility
- **Avoid overstock:** Better to sell out and wait than sit on dead inventory
- **Deal hunting:** Discogs, local shops, estate sales, bulk purchases

## Break-Even Analysis

### Fixed Costs (Monthly)

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Hosting/infrastructure | $50-100 |
| Stripe/payment fees | ~3% of revenue (variable) |
| Shipping platform integration | $50-100 |
| Misc. ops | $100-200 |
| **Total fixed** | **~$200-400/mo** |

### Break-Even Point

At ~$11 average margin per pal (blended target):
- **Monthly break-even:** ~20-40 new pals/month
- **Reached at:** ~200-400 total pals (assuming steady growth)

### Path to Profitability

1. **Months 1-3:** Operating at loss (investment phase)
2. **Months 4-6:** Approaching break-even
3. **Month 6+:** Profitable if growth sustains
4. **Year 1:** Target ~$10-15K net (reinvest in inventory & growth)
