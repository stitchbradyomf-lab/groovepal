## How It Works

### Section Title
**The Journey of a Record**

### Steps

**1. Join**
Choose your entry: Standard ($45, 2 vinyls) or Solo ($35, 1 vinyl). The Standard tier includes one vinyl for you and one for a Pal you identify — growing the network from day one.

**2. Receive**
Your welcome package arrives: curated vinyl, QR sticker sheet, and a branded outer sleeve. Physical mail. Old school.

**3. Check In**
Scan the QR code on your record to activate your Pal account. Now you're in the system.

**4. Listen**
Put it on. Sit down. Be present. This isn't background music — it's an invitation to slow down.

**5. Attach**
Add your sticker to the sleeve. Your QR code joins the record's story. Now you're part of its journey.

**6. Send Forward**
Choose someone — a friend, a family member, a curious stranger. Use our integrated shipping to generate a label. Write a note. Mail it on.

**7. Become Active**
When your recipient scans the QR and checks in, you join the Active Pal list. Now you're eligible to receive records from the network.

**8. Receive**
Active Pals get records from other Active Pals. The cycle continues. The groove moves.

## The Active Pal System

### Section Title
**Send to Receive**

### Body
Groove Pal isn't about accumulating records — it's about sharing them. To receive records from the network, you must demonstrate that you send records forward.

**How to become an Active Pal:**
1. Ship a record forward using our integrated shipping
2. Your recipient scans the QR code and checks in
3. You're now on the Active Pal list
4. Eligible to receive records from other Active Pals

**Why this works:**
- Ensures records actually travel (don't just sit on shelves)
- Rewards participation, not just payment
- Creates natural scarcity (only Active Pals receive)
- Builds trust through demonstrated action

## Shipping Made Easy

### Section Title
**We Handle the Hassle**

### Body
Shipping a record shouldn't be complicated. We've integrated everything:

**The Flow:**
1. Click "Send Forward" on the platform
2. Pay for shipping (Apple Pay, Stripe — your choice)
3. Generate a pre-paid USPS Media Mail label with QR tracking
4. Print and attach to your package
5. Drop off at USPS or schedule pickup

Cheap, easy, and trackable. The recipient scans on arrival, and you're confirmed as an Active Pal.

**Why we built this:** So you focus on the music and the connection, not the logistics.

## The Sticker System

### Section Title
**Your Mark. Your Story.**

### Body

## The Philosophy

### Section Title
**Why Slow?**

### Body
In a world of infinite scroll and instant everything, Groove Pal is intentionally slow.

It takes days for a record to arrive. It takes time to sit down and listen. It takes effort to write a note and mail it forward.

Every friction is a feature. Every delay is a gift. The wait makes the arrival meaningful. The physicality makes the connection real.
